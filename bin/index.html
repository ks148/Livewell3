Bin Assignment

Assign warehouse bins in Bulk Edit, Partial Bay, or Quick Add.
In Bulk Edit, enter Zone and Starting Bay to begin at Shelf 01 / Bin 01.
In Partial Bay, enter Zone, Bay, Shelf, and Bin to start from that exact point and continue using the same advancement logic.
In Bulk and Partial, the app now stays on the current bin until you click Done With This Bin.
